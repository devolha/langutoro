Language can be not only a final destination...

Today the common aim of learning languages is to speak and communicate with each other.

But does knowing of the tool usage really help to understand why and when it can be used?

Modern languages contains at least 30.000 words and up to 40 synonims for each word! For instance, the word "perseverance" have 32 synonims, and semantics of each word is different.

"Perseverance is about doing something despite difficulty in achieving success" - Oxford Dictionary

At the same time a word "persistance" is similar, it's a fact of continuing in opinion or course of action in spite of difficulty or opposition (note: it has more altruistic flavour and not only about success, but own principals and morals).
