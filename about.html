makes educational process more interactive and provides all the members to free education resources.

For instanse, the following video shows the collaboration between master of Japanese drums (Ei-tetsu Hayashi) and master of Shamisen (Shinn-ichi Kino-shita).

The title of the song "SHI-BU-KI" is the Japanese pronunciation of "飛沫(しぶき)" which is the Japanese splash of the sea wave.

The kanji-word "飛"-hi represents an english verb "to fly" and kanji-word "沫" means "bubbles" and "splash of the sea waves" = "flying bubbles" in Japanese.

These subtle associations on the levels of etymology and semantics of language show the mentality and structure of thought of one or another naord, their views on life.
